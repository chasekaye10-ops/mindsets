Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: MindSets stores everything on your phone. We don't collect, sell, or share your data. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party servers receiving your information.

What we collect

Nothing. MindSets does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. We have no user accounts, no email signups, and no analytics SDKs in the app.

What is stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device only, in an on-device SQLite database:

  • Your session history (type, duration, rating, timestamp)
  • Your phone-free challenges
  • Your Mindset Score and progression data
  • Your baseline focus assessment result
  • Your purchase status (whether you've unlocked premium)

This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all of this data is permanently erased.

Purchases

Purchases of the one-time premium unlock are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not receive your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the payment process. We only receive an anonymous confirmation that a purchase was made.

Notifications

If you grant permission, MindSets schedules local notifications (morning reminders, streak alerts, trial expiry reminders). These notifications are generated on your device and never sent through external servers.

Audio content

All guided audio files (NSDR meditations, walking meditations) are bundled with the app. Playing them does not connect to any external service.

Third-party services

MindSets uses no third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

The app is built with the Expo framework, which includes Expo's update service for delivering app updates. This service receives only basic technical information (app version, OS version) needed to serve updates — no personal data.

Children's privacy

MindSets is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data from children or any users. Because we collect no data, there is nothing to delete or remove.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
