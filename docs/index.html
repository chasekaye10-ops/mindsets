Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to any locked feature, and tap "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID that bought MindSets, it'll unlock instantly.

Is my data private?

Yes. MindSets stores all your session data, streaks, and progress locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. MindSets works completely offline. The only time you need internet is to download the app and process your one-time purchase.

Can I use MindSets on multiple devices?

Yes — your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so you can install MindSets on any iPhone or iPad you own. Note that progress is stored locally and doesn't sync between devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

There's no subscription to cancel. MindSets is a one-time $4.99 purchase — yours forever.
